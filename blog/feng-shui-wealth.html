Feng Shui for Wealth: 8 Proven Tips to Attract Prosperity

📅 March 20, 2024⏱️ 7 min read
Advertisement

Wealth in Feng Shui isn't just about money — it's about abundance in all forms: financial prosperity, rich relationships, good health, and meaningful opportunities. But let's be honest: most people come to Feng Shui primarily wanting to improve their finances. And the good news is, there are specific, time-tested Feng Shui practices designed exactly for this purpose.

1. Find and Activate Your Wealth Corner

Using the Bagua Map, the wealth corner of any space is the far left corner from the main entrance. Stand at your front door looking in — the back left area is your wealth zone.

Enhance this area with:

  • Purple accents: Purple is the color of wealth and nobility in Chinese culture
  • Gold or red elements: Gold represents actual wealth; red attracts good fortune
  • A healthy plant: Living, growing energy symbolizes growing wealth
  • Good lighting: Wealth energy cannot thrive in dark, neglected corners
  • Something that represents abundance to you personally

2. Fix All Leaks Immediately

In Feng Shui, water represents money. A leaking faucet, running toilet, or dripping pipe literally symbolizes money flowing away from you. This is one of the most basic and powerful Feng Shui wealth fixes — and it's completely practical. If you're literally wasting water, you're symbolically wasting wealth. Fix every leak in your home today.

3. Keep Your Stove Clean and Working

The stove represents your ability to generate wealth and nourish yourself. In Chinese culture, the kitchen stove is considered the heart of prosperity. To maximize its wealth-attracting power:

  • Keep all burners clean and in working order
  • Use all burners regularly — not just one or two
  • Place a mirror behind the stove to symbolically double your wealth
  • Keep the area around the stove clean and uncluttered

4. Place a Money Plant in the Wealth Corner

The Pachira aquatica, commonly known as the Money Tree, is one of the most popular Feng Shui wealth cures. Its braided trunk and five-lobed leaves (representing the Five Elements) make it a powerful symbol of growing prosperity. Other excellent wealth plants include:

  • Jade Plant (Crassula ovata): Its round, coin-shaped leaves earned it the nickname "Money Plant"
  • Lucky Bamboo (Dracaena sanderiana): Especially when arranged in groups of 3, 5, or 8 stalks
  • Chinese Evergreen (Aglaonema): Believed to bring good fortune

5. Use Wealth Symbols Strategically

Traditional Chinese culture has specific symbols associated with wealth. While they won't magically make you rich, they serve as powerful psychological anchors that keep your mind focused on abundance:

  • Three-Legged Toad (金蟾): The money frog with a coin in its mouth. Place it facing into your home (never facing the door, which makes wealth hop away)
  • Laughing Buddha (弥勒佛): Place facing the front door to welcome abundance
  • Chinese Coins tied with red ribbon: Hang on doorknobs or keep in your wallet
  • Wealth Vase: A beautiful vase filled with symbolic wealth items (coins, crystals, soil from a wealthy person's home, etc.) kept hidden in a cabinet
  • Dragon Turtle: Combines the power of the dragon with the stability of the turtle — excellent for career and business wealth

6. Keep Your Front Door Impeccable

The front door is called the "Mouth of Qi" — it's where all energy, including wealth energy, enters your home. A neglected front door is a blocked channel for prosperity:

  • The door should open fully and smoothly (nothing stored behind it)
  • House numbers should be clearly visible and well-lit
  • The pathway to the door should be clear and inviting
  • A clean, attractive welcome mat signals that you're ready to receive
  • Red decorations near the door attract good fortune (common during Chinese New Year)

7. Clear Clutter From Your Wallet and Desk

Your wallet is a direct reflection of your relationship with money. A wallet that's stuffed with old receipts, expired cards, and disorganized cash sends a clear message: you treat money carelessly. Keep your wallet clean, organized, and in good condition. Similarly, your desk — where you generate income — should be clear and organized, with space for new opportunities to arrive.

8. Express Gratitude for Current Wealth

This is the most overlooked Feng Shui wealth principle. The energy of appreciation attracts more to appreciate. If you're constantly focused on lack, you amplify lack. If you notice and appreciate the abundance you already have — even small things — you align yourself with the frequency of prosperity.

💡 Key Insight: Feng Shui wealth practices work on multiple levels: they improve your physical environment (less clutter, better organization), your psychological state (feeling more abundant), and — according to Chinese metaphysics — the actual energy patterns in your space. Even skeptics benefit from having a cleaner, more organized, and more intentional home.
"Wealth is not just about what you have. It's about what flows through you — and how you direct that flow." — Feng Shui Master
